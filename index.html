Zhengyu Zhou

周征宇. Incoming Research Associate at Dartmouth College

I am an incoming Research Associate at Dartmouth College. My current research interests lie broadly in machine learning theory and optimization, with a focus on matrix optimization, random matrix theory, model diagnosis, and loss landscape geometry.

Before joining Dartmouth, I completed my Ph.D. study in the School of Computer Science at Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Weiwei Liu. I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2021.

I am interested in understanding the geometry, dynamics, and reliability of modern machine learning models, especially through tools from matrix analysis, random matrix theory, and model diagnosis.

I am particularly interested in the following topics:

  • Matrix optimization and spectral methods
  • Random matrix theory in machine learning
  • Model diagnosis and model comparison
  • Loss landscape geometry
  • Optimization and generalization in deep learning
